Manage People with GTs
GT represents the member's voting power in the DAO.
Governance Tokens in total represent quorum = 100%. GTs are strictly tied to the wallets of a DAO members. They have no price, only voting power. On the DAO dashboard, you can look DAO Holders with percentage distribution of votes.
Here is a list of all actions DAO members can take with Governance tokens (GTs).
Sending GTs
Adding new members
Kick person or leave a DAO
