Roadmap
In a rapidly changing crypto world, the XDAO team is developing more and more solutions for DAOs that we believe will create the foundation for organizations of the future. We are committed to building tools to manage organizations with ease. And to work on strengthening DAO in the traditional financial sector. XDAO has plans for both the long and medium term. Some events in the roadmap can be pushed to a later or earlier date, and some new areas not mentioned before can be added as well.
