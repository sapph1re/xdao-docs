Creating Private Offer
Private offer gives the opportunity for people to buy LP tokens for special price. This offers can see only the recipient. One offer can be used only once.
After issuing LP tokens you will see "Private offers" on your DAO home page. Click "Create Private offer"
If you already have 1 or more private offers then you should click on "Private Offers" and choose the button "Create Private Offer" at the top of the list.
Fill the form and create Voting.
After the voting being executed you will see the amount of private offers. Click to "Private offers". To manage it.
Here you can see your offers. There can be more than one active private offers.
Once your private offer is used, it will be deactivated.
